    JAGBE (HOME OF KINGS)

    INTRODUCTION

    Few scattered documents were prepared and available for JAGBE CLAN in her Traditional,
    Customary, Cultural and Administrative formations.
    Till 1979, tradition and administration were focus on and linked to a spot,
    The OBA OF BENIN (BINI), the OBA OF EDO KINGDOM, OMO N’OBA N’EDO UKU-AKPOLOKPOLO.
    He is and continue to remain one of the only know Three Traditional Majesty of Traditional Institutions.
    That is the human aspect of it. All in all, everything is ordained by Almighty Osanobulua! Here,
    Jagbe Clan derived her traditional heritage.
    Therefore, Jagbe people can be regarded as Benin or Bini from home.
